Blynk announces ready-to-use blueprints for Myriota satellite-connected devices, providing pre-built dashboards, data converters, and mobile app views for the HyperPulse Developer Kit and AssetHawk tracker. The blueprints eliminate the need to build custom middleware to connect satellite data to actionable dashboards, targeting deployment sites with low or no cellular coverage.
