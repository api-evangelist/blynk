Ground Control's satellite hardware now integrates with Blynk's IoT platform, with Ground Control's Cloudloop service routing raw Iridium satellite messages directly to Blynk via HTTP Data Converter. The partnership allows developers to build fleet management dashboards, automations, and white-label apps for devices operating in remote areas beyond cellular coverage.
