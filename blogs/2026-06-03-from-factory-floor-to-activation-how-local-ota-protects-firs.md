Blynk's Local OTA (Over-The-Air) feature enables firmware updates during device provisioning via WiFi or Bluetooth before cloud connection, solving the critical problem of devices becoming outdated while in transit through supply chains. This prevents failed initial activations caused by stale firmware that can no longer connect to cloud-first update servers, improving the out-of-box experience for end users.
